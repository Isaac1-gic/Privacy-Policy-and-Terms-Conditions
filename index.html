3) Cookie & Offline Storage Notice:


Why we store data locally

MSCE G Studies Tracker is an offline-first PWA. To work without internet we store data locally on your device using browser storage (IndexedDB, localStorage). This allows you to:

Use the App offline

Save study sessions and reminders locally

Sync with GAS when online

Cookies / Analytics We may use small cookies or similar storage for session handling and non-identifying analytics to improve the app. You may clear local data or cookies from your browser, but doing so may delete unsynced study data.

Important: Always sync your data when you have internet to avoid data loss.
